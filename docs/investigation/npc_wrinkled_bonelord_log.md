## Lore Summary

### Bonelord Civilization
- Once **RULED THE ENTIRE WORLD** - had a global empire
- The **GODS DESTROYED their empire** (actively, not just abandonment)
- Advanced technology for data storage (bound spirits, beyond human comprehension)
- Books are their PRIMITIVE storage format
- Forced underground after the gods' betrayal
- The gods they served ABANDONED them (will "mourn the day")
- Very old race, given many external names; "bonelord" is an outsider label
- Their true race name is a mathematical formula that changes per viewer
- Individual names are numbers (e.g., 486486)
- Numbers are "the secret behind the scenes" - math = power over life and death
- **"Proficient in the return from death"** - can resurrect/return
- **Plan to RISE AGAIN** - dormant, not defeated
- The bonelord is studying/investigating the player (scientific curiosity)
- Plans to EAT the player after finishing research
- Humans are "good tools... once dead" (necromantic use of corpses)
- Hellgate is under Ab'Dendriel; bonelords know elves live above them

### Language 469
- Official name of the bonelord language
- **NOT spoken - BLINKED with eyes** (each digit = one eye's state)
- Based on "mathemagic" (mathematical magic)
- Requires genius-level mathematical processing to decipher
- **Tibia = 1** in bonelord language ("It's 1, not 'Tibia'")
- Zero (0) is obscene = closed eye = blindness = taboo
- **Two encoding levels:** pair-based (2-eye/books) and word-level (5-eye/spoken)

### Species Opinions
- **Minotaurs:** "Their mages are so close to the truth" (connected to mathemagics via A Prisoner)
- **Cyclops:** "Only one eye! Imagine that!" (1 eye = ultimate ugliness)
- **Humans:** "Good tools... once dead" (raw material for necromancy)
- **Elves:** "Fools with superstitious life cult" (dismissive)
- **Orcs:** "Noisy pests" (irrelevant)
- **Excalibug:** "Only inferior species need weapons" (bonelord power is mathematical, not martial)

### Cipher Analysis from NPC Speech
- NPC uses the same cipher as the 70 books in the library (written/pair format, no spaces)
- Default responses (non-keyword) are cipher fragments from master text
- Separately, the NPC has a word-level 469 message (with spaces): `4129 663 4382 12801 6639 677 35682 345 25`
- Two distinct written cipher messages observed as "filler" responses

## Eye-Count Parsing Hypothesis

See full document: `docs/investigation/eye-count-hypothesis.md`

**Core idea:** The 469 language is blinked with eyes, not spoken. Each digit = one eye's state. The number of eyes you have determines how you parse the digits:
- **2-eye parsing (humans):** read as fixed 2-digit pairs → German text (the books)
- **5-eye parsing (bonelords):** read as variable 1-5 digit words → English words (NPC speech)
- **Same data, different meaning** depending on the viewer's eye count

This explains:
- Why the bonelord's name "486486" changes per viewer (2 eyes: NTM, 3 eyes: 486-486, 5 eyes: 48648-6)
- Why 0 is obscene (eye state 0 = closed = blind)
- Why books are "primitive storage" (forced into 2-digit pair format)
- Why the Honeminas formula uses 5-element vectors (one per eye)
- Why "only entities with enough eyes" can use the language
