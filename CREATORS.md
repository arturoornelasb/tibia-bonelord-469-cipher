## The Story Arc

1. **The mystery** — 70 books of pure digits, unsolved since ~2000
2. **The breakthrough** — It's German, not English. Homophonic substitution, not math.
3. **CipSoft's tricks** — Removed digits from half the books, anagrammed proper nouns
4. **The decoded text** — A bonelord funeral poem about King Salzberg and ancient ruins
5. **What's still hidden** — 5 unsolved proper nouns, in-game locations to verify
6. **The novel technique** — BoLWP, a new cryptanalytic method born from this research
